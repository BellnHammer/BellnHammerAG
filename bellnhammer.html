FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your project.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the Pacific Northwest, primarily throughout Washington state. Contact us to confirm availability in your specific area.

Are you licensed, bonded, and insured?

Yes — Bell & Hammer is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We provide documentation on request, no questions asked.

How does your estimate process work?

Fill out our form or give us a call. We'll schedule an on-site visit, talk through your goals, and deliver a detailed written estimate — zero pressure, zero obligation.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Absolutely. We handle all permitting and coordinate inspections so the paperwork never falls on you.

How long does a typical remodel take?

Depends on scope, but we set realistic timelines upfront and stick to them. You'll have a clear schedule before any work begins.

What sets you apart from other contractors?

Communication. We give you daily updates, honest timelines, and no surprises. You'll always know what's happening, what's next, and what it costs.
