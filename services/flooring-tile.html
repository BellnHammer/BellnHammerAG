Clean installs from subfloor to finish. Hardwood, LVP, ceramic, porcelain — laid level with tight seams and clean transitions.

What We Do

Level Floors, Clean Cuts

Flooring is one of those things everyone notices when it's wrong — lippage in the tile, gaps in the planks, transitions that catch your toe. We install floors that lay flat, fit tight, and look right from every angle.

Whether it's luxury vinyl plank in a rental unit or large-format porcelain tile in a master bath, we start with proper subfloor prep, self-leveling compound where needed, and appropriate underlayment for the material. The foundation matters as much as what goes on top.

We handle the full scope — demo of existing flooring, subfloor repair, material layout, installation, transitions, and trim. If it touches the floor, we've got it.

FAQ

Common Questions

How long does flooring installation take?+

A typical room takes 1-2 days. Whole-house installs run about a week depending on material and subfloor condition.

Do I need to remove my existing flooring first?+

We handle all demo and disposal. In some cases we can install over existing flooring if the subfloor is sound.

What's the difference between LVP and hardwood?+

LVP is waterproof, lower cost, and great for high-traffic areas. Hardwood has a premium look and feel but needs more care. We install both and can help you choose.
