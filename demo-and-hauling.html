FAQ

Common Questions

How big is the dump bed?+

6 feet wide by 8 feet long with built walls. That's roughly 4 cubic yards at full capacity — enough for most single-room demo jobs, roofing tear-offs, or yard cleanouts.

Do you load the material or do I need to?+

Either way works. If you need us to load, we charge $65/hr per person for loading assistance. If it's already piled and ready, we'll get it on the trailer and go.

Can you do same-day pickup?+

When our schedule allows, yes. Same-day and rush bookings have a $50 surcharge. Call us and we'll let you know what's available.

What about heavy stuff like concrete or dirt?+

We haul it, but heavy materials incur a $50–75 surcharge because dump fees are charged by weight. We'll confirm the cost before we load so there are no surprises.

Do you haul hazardous materials?+

We can handle some special items like tires and paint with a $40–80 surcharge. For anything truly hazardous (asbestos, chemicals), we'll refer you to a licensed specialist.
